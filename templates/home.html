The Problem

Getting an avatar for a flickr user is a royal pain! If you have their username you basically have to scrape their profile page to get their ID number in order to get their avatar. Turns out after you've done this once you realize it's more trouble than it's worth.

The Solution

Inspired by tweetimag.es the solution is to have a service that can do all that hard work for you. All you need to do is plug a username into our url and out comes an avatar!

For example if you wanted to see Jannis Leidel's flickr avatar all you need to do is: 

<img src="http://flickrimag.es/i/jannis.jpg" />
